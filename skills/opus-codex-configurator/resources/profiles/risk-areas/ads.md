# Risk Profile: ads

## Category

risk-area

## Rule

Ad SDKs, rewarded ads, interstitials, and monetization triggers are medium/high risk. Isolate SDKs and validate unavailable/skipped/completed states.

## Codex default restriction

Do not modify this area unless the Codex Task Packet explicitly authorizes it.

## Opus review focus

Check scope, validation evidence, rollback path, and whether human approval is required.
