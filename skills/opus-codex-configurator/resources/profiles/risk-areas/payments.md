# Risk Profile: payments

## Category

risk-area

## Rule

Billing, subscriptions, purchase validation, entitlements, refunds, and payment provider integrations are high-risk. Require approval and evidence for edge cases.

## Codex default restriction

Do not modify this area unless the Codex Task Packet explicitly authorizes it.

## Opus review focus

Check scope, validation evidence, rollback path, and whether human approval is required.
