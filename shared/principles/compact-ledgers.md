# Compact Ledgers

Do not rely on full historical task files for normal context.

Maintain compact ledgers:

- `docs/ai/execution-log/latest.md` — latest accepted tasks only;
- `docs/ai/execution-log/archive/YYYY-MM.md` — monthly summaries;
- `docs/product/backlog/index.md` — recently completed task IDs and titles only;
- `docs/ai/execution-state.md` — current delivery status.

A compact ledger should preserve:

- task ID;
- result;
- validation outcome;
- changed areas;
- unresolved follow-up.

It should not duplicate full Codex reports.
